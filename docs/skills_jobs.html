Senior Project Manager

In order to take our project to the next level we will need to have our time and roles managed and organised. If we were to hire a Senior Project Manager, we would be able to be more organised and our work ethic would be considerably more efficient then if we did not have someone overseeing our work. With a senior Manager on our team we will have someone that our team can consult if they have any questions or problems. They will need the ability to multitask, in order to focus on multiple problems and will need good communication skills so that they can create an efficient working environment. A strong leadership mindset will also be required to lead and maintain the group.
